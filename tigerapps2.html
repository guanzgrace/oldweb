Course Planning

Plan your schedule and get notifications when spots open up.

Recal.io

Select your courses and export to the enrollment worksheet or Google Calendar. Created by Naphat Sanguansin '16, Dyland Xue '15, and Maxim Zaslavsky '17.

Princeton Pounce

Recieve text or email notifications when free spots open up in classes. Created by Jeremy Cohen '16 and Zach Koerbel '16.

Wintersession

Signup for Wintersession classes. Signups for Wintersession 2017 are currently closed.

Directories

Find students on campus.

Tigerbook

A unified directory of Princeton undergraduates that incorporates athletics data, university directory data, and student organization data. Created by Hansen Qian ’16, Ivo Crnkovic-Rubsamen ’15 and Rohan Sharma ’14. Improvements by Adam Libresco ’19.

College Facebook Search

A directory of Princeton undergraduates with more specific advanced search features. Use tags like #name or #major in order to specify what categories you want to search in. Created by Ben Parks '17.

Campus Life

Find clubs, trade goods, and come prepared for Room Draw.

Student Room Guide

An easy way to search for your next year's rooms. All rooms for each draw group are mapped onto a map of campus, and you can view all buildings' floor plans. Add rooms to a "room queue" and be alerted when a room is taken by e-mail or text message. Developed in Spring 2012 by Joshua Giles '13, Willa Chen '13, Lily Healey '13, Kevin Mantel '13, and John O'Neill '13.

TigerTrade

TigerTrade makes it easy for members of the Princeton University community to buy, sell, and exchange stuff. You can offer items for sale, rent, or exchange, and you can also list items you're looking to buy. Developed in 2011 by Rodrigo Menezes '13.
