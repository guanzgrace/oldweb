USG TigerApps
Student-built, student-maintained apps built over the years, designed to improve your campus experience. Managed and maintained by the Princeton USG IT Committee. If you have any questions or comments, or are interested in joining, please contact us!
Princeton Pounce
Princeton Pounce
Recieve text or email notifications when free spots open up in classes. Created by Jeremy Cohen '16 and Zach Koerbel '16.
TigerTrade
TigerTrade
Buy, sell, and exchange "stuff". Offer items for sale, rent, or exchange, or list items you're looking to buy. Developed in 2011 by Rodrigo Menezes '13, USG IT Chair.
Student Room Guide
Student Room Guide
An easy way to search for a dorm room. View maps and floor plans. Add rooms to a "room queue." Be alerted when a room is taken by e-mail or text. Developed in Spring 2012 by Joshua Giles '13, Willa Chen '13, Lily Healey '13, Kevin Mantel '13, and John O'Neill '13 for COS 333.
ReCal
ReCal
Easy course selection tool. Developed by Naphat Sanguansin '16, Dyland Xue '16, and Maxim Zaslavsky '17.
Tigerbook
Tigerbook
Directory of Princeton undergrads incorporating athletics and student organization data with advanced search and visualization functions. Developed by Hansen Qian '16, Ivo Crnkovic-Rubsamen '15 and Rohan Sharma '14 with improvements by Adam Libresco '19.
College Facebook Search
College Facebook Search
Directory of Princeton undergrads with advanced search in specific categories such as dorm, major, class, etc. Developed by Ben Parks '17.
